Welcome to 'Super Pang'
A pong clone by Aidan Nabass

Pang is played by 2 players with control of paddles. You get points by knocking the ball past the other players paddle.
First to 10 points wins.
The high score screen keeps track of the highest losing score.


Controls
++++++++++
Player 1 (Left Side)
 Move Up - 'W'
 Move Down - 'S'

Player 2 (Right Side)
 Move Up - 'Up Arrow'
 Move Down - 'Down Arrow'
